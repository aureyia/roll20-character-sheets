Announcements

Changes in

  • Added Dwarf specific Nonweapon and Dwarven detection proficiencies from The Complete Book of Dwarves
  • Added Weapons and Proficiencies from The Complete Barbarian's Handbook
  • Added Spells and Proficiencies from The Complete Book of Necromancers
  • Added Weapons, Spells, and Proficiencies from The Complete Ninja's Handbook
  • Added Sphere extensions from The Complete Druid's Handbook
  • Added Sphere extensions from The Complete Book of Necromancers
  • Added introductory text for Wizards and Priest, on how to create Scrolls, Potions, and other Magical items
    • The amount of information is intentionally kept sparse as the in depth details are for the DM only.
  • Added fields to keep track of Psionic Primary and Secondary Disciplines
  • Added help text for Psionic Disciplines
  • Added fields to track temporary attack, damage, and speed buffs
  • Added buttons do deal critical damage, to the standard weapons. This follows the first description of the Dungeon Master Guide
  • Added rerolls for Arquebus damage when a 10 is rolled, as per the PHB. Readd your Arquebus to get the update stats
  • Added target size to output text when rolling damage (Small/Medium or Large)
  • Added styling for the sheet settings table
  • Fixed the Grenade Miss button when using Holy water
  • Corrected the output text when using Holy water, splash damage
